How It Started

A Dream Born From Love

Loving Homes was founded in 2018 by a team of dog lovers who couldn't find a place that truly felt like home for their own pets. Every hotel felt cold, clinical, or overcrowded.

So we built our own. A place where every dog has their own cosy room, individual attention, and a team that genuinely cares — not just professionally, but personally.

Today, Loving Homes is one of the most trusted dog hotels in the region, recognised for our exceptional care standards and the deep bonds we build with every dog who stays with us.
